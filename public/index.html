Welcome to ChemE Car.

In pursuit of a true multidisciplinary, hands-on experience, undergraduate chemical engineering students founded University of Toronto’s ChemE Car Design Team. We research, design, build, integrate, and optimize different systems to create a small, autonomous, chemically powered car every year. If you are interested in working in a safe laboratory environment, learning about innovative electrochemistry or reaction kinetics, building cars, or even working behind the scenes to manage resources, logistics, and events-planning, then ChemE Car is the team to join.

Mission

The club’s mission is to offer authentic multidisciplinary experience that promotes students’ cross-functionality across different engineering fields. We commit to becoming:

  1. Technically Competent in optimally designing, building, and integrating components to perfect a chemically-powered, environmentally friendly, autonomous car
  2. Resilient Leaders with a can-do attitude to work through challenges and support others through challenges by taking initiative to propel the team forward.
  3. Effective Communicators to express our thoughts and ideas clearly and concisely.

Competition

We compete in American Institute for Chemical Engineers’ (AIChE) Chem-E-Car Regional Competition™ against other universities from all over the world. You can find out more about competition rules here. Here is a video of the competition environment.

Vision

Upscaling a perfected small car model into an environmentally friendly, one-passenger-sized, autonomous vehicle.

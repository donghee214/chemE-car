Circuitry and Programming

Circuitry Team is in charge of controlling the logic of the operations in the car. The control of the motor is based on the input from a sensor, and after analysis, a decision is made on whether the power supply should stop powering the motor or not. You will be designing circuits from scratch, choosing your own components based on their datasheets, integrating programming and electronics, circuit testing for optimization, designing and manufacturing of printed circuit boards (PCB).

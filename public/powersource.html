Power Source

PST is responsible for building a mechanism that can reliably power small car models being built. In Power Source Team, you will learn more about electrochemistry and how it can be applied to provide power to a small scale car. Solid State Batteries is the team’s focus for 2016/2017. The team will be researching, developing CAD models, building and testing different powering models in a laboratory setting, as well as optimizing and troubleshooting designs.

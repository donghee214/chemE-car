R&D-Power Source

R&D Power Source is responsible for creating a new source of energy for our car in parallel with the original Power Source Team. The problems that R&D Power Source Team attempt to solve are very open ended, stimulating creativity and promoting troubleshooting skills. Thermo-Electric Generators is the focus of 2016/2017 team. It will be used in conjunction with an endothermic and exothermic reaction to provide energy. The skills obtained every year are different, due to the changing nature of the project. Skills to be developed this year include but not limited to; CAD modeling, reaction kinetics and general lab skills.

R&D- Stopping Mechanism

R&D Stopping Mechanism will be developing an alternate stopping mechanism to stop the car accurately on competition day. Work in the lab will involve but is not limited to extensive testing on factors that affect the reaction rate and improving on the accuracy of the mechanism by iterating on reactor design and the experimental protocol. The 2016/17 team will focus on the catalysed hydrogen peroxide decomposition reaction. Oxygen gas evolved in this reaction translates to a measurable pressure/volume change. The main goal of the project is to know what exact starting material concentration is needed to achieve a fixed pressure/volume change by a specified time. Skills and knowledge to be gained include kinetics of the reaction, data collection and analysis, general lab and design skills.

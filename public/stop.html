Stopping Mechanism

The stopping mechanism team is responsible for stopping the car model accurately using a physical property of a chemical reaction (i.e. no mechanical brakes allowed). Given the importance of calibrating the reaction to provide accurate times, you will gain extensive theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience in reaction kinetics, statistical analysis, and general chemical laboratory work.

Mechanical Design

MDT is responsible for designing and building the chassis of the car. On this team, you will learn to design components for the car, use CAD modelling software and basic machining processes to assemble the car. Once the car construction is completed, you will have to troubleshoot and optimize the design to function smoothly and reliably.
